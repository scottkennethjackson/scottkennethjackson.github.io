About Us

Welcome to Main Street Tattoo, where ink meets narrative in the heart of creativity. Tucked away on the edge of the The Village, our studio breathes life into stories through the artistry of our diverse team. From traditional to avant-garde, our talented tattooists, led by the visionary Mai, transform skin into living canvases. With roots in global aesthetics, Main Street Tattoo embodies a fusion of tradition and contemporary expression. Step into our haven, where every tattoo is a chapter, and Main Street becomes a gallery of personal narratives. Join us in the symphony of ink, where tales unfold under the skilled hands of our passionate artists.

Meet the Team

Mai

Mai's art is a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity. With delicate precision, she weaves cultural tapestries, creating tattoos that resonate with both personal narratives and the echoes of ancient traditions.

Dirk

Hailing from the gritty streets of Brooklyn, Dirk's art is a fusion of urban edge and timeless symbolism. Each piece telling a raw, unfiltered story—a visual symphony that echoes the pulse of the city’s heartbeat.

Elouise

Dancing between ethereal and edgy, Elouise's art is a testament to her eclectic influences. With a needle as her paintbrush, she crafts visual poetry on skin, infusing each piece with a touch of whimsy and a dash of rebellion.
